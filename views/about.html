The Atom Review supports visionary young artists, while serving as an instrument of art discovery. Every weekday, we showcase one new artist by exhibiting his or her work alongside a short interview. Our mission is to empower and celebrate the filmmakers, visual artists, musicians, performers, and writers that enrich the world with their life's work. We will be rolling out a series of tools in the coming months that enable artists to better support and promote themselves in their creative pursuits.
